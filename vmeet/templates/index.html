Stay Connected!

Lockdowns can be hard, but fun needn’t be. In this global pandemic, we are locked down in our own space with less opportunities to socialize which can negatively impact our mental health and thus leading to lower productivity. Our project aims to connect you with your like-minded colleagues. Do you want take a break after a long meeting and game with some colleagues? Do you miss working out at the VMware gym and taking classes by your favorite instructors? Do you miss performing at VMware events? We have a platform for you, our web-application provides multiple spaces to cater to these needs at your leisure. We aim to break the communication barrier caused by the pandemic by creating networking opportunities that improve mental health by engaging in community building activities. Our idea focuses on the Community aspect of VMware’s EPIC2 values.
